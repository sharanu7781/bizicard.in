About Me

Shivkala Mudaliar - Profile
A Social Entrepreneur, trainer with over 23+ years of administrative management experience working with MNC & Corporates like BPL Mobile, ABN Ambro Equities, Standard Chartered Bank, Nexus Venture Partners.
  • Shortlisted among the top 100 Women Entrepreneurs in India by Niti Aayog for Women Transforming India Awards 2019.

  • A mentor at Cherie Blair (Ex Prime Minister Tony Blair’s wife) Foundation, UK. Mentoring women across the globe for their business.

  • Trustee Member of Navdrushti NGO established in 1996 with an objective to control the Child Malnutrition, Morbidity and Mortality in tribal belt of Palghar dist., Maharashtra.
Founder of Things Etc
A social enterprise working with 200+ Tribal Artist, 32 NGOs and 800+ women from various Self Help Groups across India since 2013.

Working towards revival or Indian art forms and bringing sustainable livelihood opportunities for local artisans. Through its online and offline store we help create a local, domestic and international market for all products made by the artisans. With over 1000+ clients are top clients are Forest Department of Maharashtra, IIT Mumbai, Delhi Government Education Department, India Post, SNDT University, Pune University etc.
Things Etc has been recognized for its work over the years, to mention a few:
  • Lokmat (India's No 1 Marathi Newspaper) - Women Entrepreneurship Award 2018.

  • Maharashtra Small Scale Industries recognized us as one of the top startups of 2018 and featured us in their Coffee Table Book.
Co Founder Woloo - India's first Women's Powder Room
Woloo is an innovatively designed hygienic sanitation space with a cafe and convenience store with women centric products in case of emergency. Also equipped with CCTV cameras, Wi-Fi, centrally air-conditioned.

A ‘For Women by Women’ initiative focusing on hygiene dignity of every woman of all ages and diversity.
