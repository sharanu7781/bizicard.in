Dr. Jaai Rane joined Smiling Stars Dental Clinic for Children, Mulund in October 2020 as an Associate Pediatric Dentist. 
She has completed her specialization in Pediatric Dentistry from DY Patil University, Navi Mumbai in 2017, during which she was trained under Dr. Uma Dixit. 
Since then, she has practiced in exclusive Pediatric practice independently. 
Dr. Jaai has a calm approach that helps in putting our little patients at ease during their dental treatment. Our patients adore her due to her friendly nature.
