Mala Mansukhani Fashion Designer, Philanthropist, Author

Mala Mansukhani is a fashion designer, a philanthropist, an author and the founder of a unique organization called, “Grow Younger”. Grow Younger is Mala’s most proud endeavour and it’s focused towards promoting fitness and good health among women above the age of 50 years.

Her mission is to spread the “goodness of healthy living and exercise” by teaching easy steps to impact people’s lives positively and drastically. She has been bestowed with many accolades and awards for her work towards the society and touching lives of thousands of women across the country.

About me

Mala Mansukhani is a fashion designer, a philanthropist, an author and the founder of a unique organization called, “Grow Younger”. Grow Younger is Mala’s most proud endeavour and it’s focused towards promoting fitness and good health among women above the age of 50 years.

She has won many awards and has been covered by few magazines and newspapers like Times of India, Zoom Delhi, Mumbai Global, Hindi & Marathi. She also featured on shows on Radio Magic 106.4 FM and Jee Le Zaraa.

She has worked with NASEOH for 18 months for special abled children, Access Life NGO, Cancer Children etc. She keeps visiting on and off, Bal Anand World Children Welfare Trust, Earth Angels Welfare Foundation, CPAA (Cancer Patient Aid Association). She has also done workshops with them on how to keep yourself fit even while working in 9 to 5 job.
